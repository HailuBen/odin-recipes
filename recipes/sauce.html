Sweet Cranberry and Soy Chili Oil Sauce

Back home or Coca-cola and Soy Chicken drumsticks or Pancakes or Donuts


Description from Little Fat Boy

"A sweet, spicy, and tangy sauce that goes perfect with meat or dumplings.
It's punchy sweetness comes from cranberries, while chili oil and soy balance out the sauce that makes it perfect for savory dishes.
Yields 1 container, Takes 10 minutes"
-Frankie

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 whole scallions, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar or white vinegar (2 teaspoons if you like it more acidic!)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili crisp oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 teaspoons maple syrup or honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons canned cranberry sauce
Back to top of page

Steps:

    Prep sauce:
  1. Dice scallions and finely grate ginger. Cut garlic into thin slices.
  2. In a saucepan on medium heat, add a tablespoon of olive oil. Add ginger, scallions, and garlic and lightly saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Turn the heat to low, then mix in the remaining sauce ingredients until they're all incorporated.
  4. Serve on savory dishes like meat or dumplings.
  5. Enjoy!

    6. Notes: For chili crisp oil, I love using the classic Lao Gan Ma. Fly by Jing and Su Chili Crisp are also great options. Or just use your favorite chili crisp oil. All will create slightly varied but still delicious sauces.
