Description from Little Fat Boy

"A sweet, spicy, and tangy sauce that goes perfect with meat or dumplings.
It's punchy sweetness comes from cranberries, while chili oil and soy balance out the sauce that makes it perfect for savory dishes.
Yields 1 container, Takes 10 minutes"
-Frankie

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 whole scallions, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar or white vinegar (2 teaspoons if you like it more acidic!)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili crisp oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 teaspoons maple syrup or honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons canned cranberry sauce

Steps:

    Batter:
  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a bowl
  2. Make a well in the center of the dry mixture and pour in milk, 1 egg, and melted butter
  3. Mix until smooth
    4. Cooking
  4. Head a lightly oiled griddle, frying pan, or other cooking surface over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour approx. 1/4 cup of batter onto griddle
  6. Wait until bottom is browned to your liking, and flip.
  7. once both sides are cooked, transfer to wire rack to cool and evenly distribute the heat, or simply serve on a plate with appropriate toppings.
  8. Enjoy!
