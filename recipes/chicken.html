Sweet and Salty Coca-Cola Soy Glazed Chicken Drumsticks

Description from Little Fat Boy

"A pot of drumsticks slowly braised in a sweet/salty/funky sauce made of Coca-cola (a dad favorite for marinade), soy sauce, black vinegar, and hit of fish sauce.
The resulting pot of drumsticks becomes so tender and sticky sweet full of concentrated sauce that it begs to be eaten with a fresh bowl of white rice.
8-10 drumsticks, Takes 3 hrs"
-Frankie

Ingredients:

    Chicken
  • 2.5 pounds of chicken drumsticks

    • Dry Rub
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon grated garlic (6 cloves)
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger

    • Braising Liquid
  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons black vinegar or rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 cup Coca-cola
Back to top of page

Steps:

    Prep dry rub and chicken
  1. In a bowl combine brown sugar, granulated sugar, kosher salt, onion powder, pepper, garlic, and grond ginger
  2. Stir the concotion

  3. Add drumsticks to dry rub
  4. Massage each drumstick in rub
  5. Cover bowl with drumsticks and refrigerate for 30 minutes

    6. Braising Chicken
  6. Preheat oven to 275°F or 135°C
  7. Add soy sauce, honey, black vinegar, fish sauce, and Coca-cola to oven safe pot and mix.
  8. Toss drumsticks in mixture
  9. Place pot in oven
  10. Wait 2 1/2 hours (or 1 hour for every pound of meat you're braising)
  11. Remove chicekn from oven and serve with scallions and white rice
  12. Enjoy!
