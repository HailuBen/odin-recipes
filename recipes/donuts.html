"1 minute, 2 ingredient no Yeast DONUTS recipe! Learn how to make these EASY DONUTS!"

Ingredients:

    For the donuts
  • 140g (1 cup) Self-raising flour
  • 190g (6.5 oz + ½ tbsp) Any plain yogurt
    • Substitutions (for flour and/or yogurt)
  • Yogurt can be substituted for milk BUT add only 112 g (½ cup) of milk.
  • 40 g (1 cup) of plain flour + 1½ tsp baking powder
    • or
  • 140 g (1 cup) of plain flour + ½ tsp baking soda

    • For the glaze
  • 250 g (2 cups) Powdered sugar
  • 75 g (⅔ stick) Butter
  • 3 - 4 tbsp Milk, depending how thin you like your glaze
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder or melted chocolate
Back to top of page

Steps:

    For the donuts:
  1. In a bowl combine self-raising flour and yogurt.
  2. Pour the sticky mixture into a piping bag. Do not add extra flour.
  3. Bring the oil to 170 °C (340 °F) and fry the donuts in batches until golden.
  4. Toss the donuts in the glaze.
    5. For the glaze:
  5. Melt the butter and add the powdered sugar and milk.
  6. Add cocoa powder if desired.
